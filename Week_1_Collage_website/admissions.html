Admission Procedure

Admission into UG and PG programmes of the Institution is based on State level Common Entrance Tests conducted by the Government of Andhra Pradesh.

Undergraduate Engineering programme admissions are through EAMCET / ECET. MBA admissions are based on ICET, and M.Tech admissions are through PGECET and GATE.

Eligibility

The eligibility criteria for admission into UG & PG programmes are as per the norms approved by the Government of Andhra Pradesh from time to time.

Categories

Category - A

70% of the seats are based on EAMCET/ICET/PGECET or GATE for M.Tech admissions.

Category - B

30% of the seats are filled under Management/NRI quota as per the norms of APSCHE.

Lateral Entry Programmes

10% of the seats are reserved for Lateral Entry based on ECET and vacancy seats from the previous year.
